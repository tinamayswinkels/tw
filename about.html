ABOUT US

Tropica Weddings is a creative team of wedding photographers, videographers & designers based in Melbourne.

Our goal is to create your ultimate dream wedding by capturing your special day in the style it deserves.
Having worked all over the world, from Indonesia to Greece, our creative team will offer you their knowledge, creativity and experience to help
inspire you and design a well executed and memorable wedding day. When it comes to your wedding day, we are known for our candid
and natural photographic style that ensures we capture every moment. Paired with bespoke wedding stationery from engagement
invitations to wedding thank you notes, Tropica Weddings will help you set the tone for a day you will never forget.
